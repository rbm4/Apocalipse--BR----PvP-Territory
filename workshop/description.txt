[h1]Apocalipse [BR] - PvP Territory[/h1]

Faction territory warfare for Project Zomboid Build 42 multiplayer servers. Mark parts of the map as conquest zones, let factions fight over them, reward the groups that hold territory, and give admins in-game tools to shape the conflict.

[h2]Core PvP Features[/h2]
[list]
[*]Faction-based capture zones with live owner, attacker, progress, contested, and protected states.
[*]Capture progress is driven by faction members standing inside the zone.
[*]Defenders and allied factions can contest attacks and pause capture progress.
[*]Multiple attacking factions in one zone force a contested state.
[*]Offline raid protection for territory owners, with optional expiry after inactivity.
[*]Abandonment cleanup can return inactive faction territories to Neutral.
[*]Global radio-style alerts and local player alerts for attacks, captures, abandoned zones, and payday rewards.
[*]Multiplayer synchronization through ModData for zone definitions, ownership, diplomacy, and status.
[/list]

[h2]Player Interface[/h2]
[list]
[*]On-screen capture progress overlay when standing inside a territory.
[*]Faction control panel with territory lists, ownership status, map display, and diplomacy actions.
[*]World map rendering for territory areas, using faction colors where available.
[*]Context-menu access to the faction territory panel.
[*]Faction zone moodle with tooltip feedback when territory buffs are active.
[/list]

[h2]Admin Zone Management[/h2]
[list]
[*]Create custom conquest zones directly in-game.
[*]Draw zones on the map and define names, coordinates, Z-level ranges, and zone types.
[*]Rename, delete, teleport to, and change the type of custom zones.
[*]Link reward containers to territories through the world object context menu.
[*]Custom zones integrate with the required ApocalipseBR_Regioes hot-region system.
[/list]

[h2]Rewards, Buffs, and Progression[/h2]
[list]
[*]Daily faction salary payout at 9:00 AM for online faction members.
[*]Salary scales by the number of zones owned by the player's faction.
[*]Linked reward crates restock every 24 in-game hours for the faction that owns the zone.
[*]Configurable salary item, salary amount, crate reward item, crate amount, and bonus loot.
[*]Custom reward item lists support comma- or semicolon-separated item IDs.
[*]Server-authoritative passive XP and survival buffs for players in zones owned by their faction or an allied faction.
[*]Custom territory perk tree with themed zone perks.
[/list]

[h2]Diplomacy[/h2]
[list]
[*]Faction leaders can send alliance invites to other factions.
[*]Alliances are mutual once accepted.
[*]Allied factions count as defenders for contested-zone logic.
[*]Invites can be accepted, declined, or cleared through the faction interface.
[*]Alliance creation is limited so each faction can keep one active ally.
[/list]

[h2]Server Configuration[/h2]
[list]
[*]Capture time.
[*]Capture speed multiplier.
[*]Decay speed multiplier.
[*]Capture announcements.
[*]Daily salary reward type and amount.
[*]Passive XP amount.
[*]Offline protection and expiry time.
[*]Faction abandonment timeout.
[*]Custom salary reward items.
[*]Custom crate reward items.
[*]Crate loot amount.
[*]Bonus crate loot toggle.
[/list]

[h2]Requirements[/h2]
[list]
[*]Project Zomboid Build 42.19 or newer.
[*]Requires ApocalipseBR_Regioes.
[/list]

[h2]Server Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*]Designed for multiplayer PvP environments.
[*]Admins should tune capture speed, protection, rewards, and zone placement around their server economy and raid rules.
[*]Hardcoded zones are disabled by default; admins can create custom zones in-game.
[/list]
